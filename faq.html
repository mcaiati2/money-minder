Frequently Asked Questions

What does this application do?

This is a budgeting application that you can use for storing and tracking your finances. You can set a monthly budget and add new expenses. Your statement will update to show all of your expenses as you go, and stores them for you to reference in the future as well.

How do I input a new expense?

On the home page, click the "Add New Expense" button on the home page. You will be prompted by a pop-up to add a text description, the cost, and the date of the new expense. When you choose to confirm and submit the expense, it will then automatically appear in your statement.

Where can I see my list of expenses I have added?

Whenever you - our lovely user - have entered a new expense, MoneyMinder adds it to your current statement. This is visible directly on the home page. You can click the "Current Statement" button or simply scroll further down the page to view this statement.

Where can I view my user profile?

Simply click the dropdown menu on the top right of the screen and select the "Profile" option.

And how about my user settings? Where can I view those?

Again, simply click the dropdown menu on the top right of the screen. Select "Settings" this time and you will be able to view and change your settings.

Would MoneyMinder ever sell my personal data?

MoneyMinder will never sell your personal data.
